Reverse-engineer a landing page or advertorial for reuse as a direct response template.

Page URL or file: $ARGUMENTS

Analyze and output:

## 1. Page Structure (in order)
List every section with a descriptive name and its order on the page.

## 2. Section Breakdown
For each section:
- **Purpose:** What it does from a direct response / CRO perspective
- **Copy formula:** The specific formula used (e.g., PAS, AIDA, social proof stack, fear/solution/CTA)
- **Key elements:** Headlines, subheads, visuals, CTAs, trust signals

## 3. Reusable Template
Strip out brand-specific content and output a fill-in-the-blank template version of the full page structure that can be reused for any brand.

## 4. What's Working (CRO Audit)
Identify the 3-5 highest-leverage elements driving conversions on this page.
